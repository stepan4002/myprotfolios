How I work the cycle

Discovery to close, in five steps.

The same operating motion across all three ventures — what changes is the buyer, not the discipline.

01
Discovery
Listen first. Map the real need vs. the stated brief. Decide whether there’s a fit before pitching anything.
02
Proposal
Tight written proposal — scope, options, prices, timeline. Built for the buyer’s decision committee, not for me.
03
Negotiation
Multilingual negotiation, terms and contracting. Comfortable holding price while finding shape concessions.
04
Delivery
I stay in the loop through delivery — the operator and the seller are the same person, so promises hold.
05
Renewal
Post-sale care, references, repeat business and partner referrals — the cheapest pipeline I have.
