Chapter 02 ─

Three ventures.
Three distinct motions.

CADEMA
Co-Founder · 2019 — present
B2B / B2C
Made-to-order manufacturing
Long-cycle consultative selling

Bespoke wooden sheds, garden structures and buildings, sold across Czechia and Slovakia. Long-cycle consultative selling — site visits, custom quotes, 3D-configurator demos, multi-week negotiations with families and small businesses making a five-figure decision.

To compress quote cycles, I built the 3D configurator itself, a Czech-language voice agent that handles inbound qualification, and a parametric Blender pipeline that turns each quote into a render in minutes — not days. Founder mentality on cost, throughput and audit trails.

A cultural-arts institute in the Gemer region of Slovakia. Sponsorship sales to regional brands, ticketed events, artisan partner agreements, institutional grants. Selling intangible value — experience, audience, cultural cachet — to brands, municipalities and EU funds.

2025 footprint: 156 events delivered, 20,000+ participants. Multi-stakeholder, multi-language, multi-budget. The discipline is single source-of-truth briefs feeding budgets, marketing, day-of run sheets and post-event reports.

Chapter 04 ─

Discovery to renewal,
in five steps.

i
Discovery
Listen first. Map the real need vs. the stated brief. Decide whether there's a fit before pitching.
ii
Proposal
Tight written proposal — scope, options, prices, timeline. Built for the buyer's decision committee, not for me.
iii
Negotiation
Multilingual terms and contracting. Hold price; find shape concessions. Comfortable letting silence do work.
iv
Delivery
I stay in the loop through delivery — operator and seller are the same person, so promises hold.
v
Renewal
Post-sale care, references, repeat business, partner referrals — the cheapest pipeline there is.
